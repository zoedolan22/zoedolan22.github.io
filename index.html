About

Five years of building
work that holds.

I started in content marketing and grew into a full-spectrum Digital Strategist role. Along the way I've built campaigns, managed content teams, written the copy, owned the analytics, and reported back on what worked. I've developed a clear point of view: good strategy requires real insight into the business problem, a channel mix built around the audience, and the kind of internal rigour that makes execution possible.

I currently manage a multi-client B2B and B2C portfolio spanning finance, telecoms, NGOs, retail, travel, and luxury consumer brands, including Allan Gray, Finbond, Herotel Business, Funda Wande, HID Global, and Global Accommodation. I own end-to-end digital strategy for each, including content and channel frameworks, campaign planning, paid media across Google Ads (including Performance Max), Meta, and LinkedIn, analytics and reporting, and brand positioning.

As Head of Strategy, I act as a strategic partner to clients and stakeholders, translating business goals into clear direction for teams and mentoring team members along the way. I'm also AI-fluent, using tools like these to support strategy, content, and reporting workflows. Based in the Western Cape, working with clients across South Africa and internationally.
